## Section 1: About the Role
The Star Entertainment Group is looking for a proactive Data Analyst to join the Group Gaming Logistics & Analysis team. This role is pivotal in transforming complex data into actionable insights to optimize operational performance and enhance guest experiences across their world-class integrated resorts.

## Section 2: Responsibilities
* Extract, transform, and load (ETL) data from various sources to support strategic business decision-making.
* Develop and maintain automated reports and interactive dashboards (Data Visualization) to track key performance indicators (KPIs).
* Collaborate with business stakeholders to identify data needs and provide predictive analysis on market and customer trends.
* Ensure data integrity and consistency across all reporting platforms and data repositories.
* Identify opportunities for process improvement and operational efficiency based on data-driven findings.

## Section 3: Key Skills & Requirements

### Technical Skills
* **SQL:** Advanced proficiency in writing complex queries and data manipulation.
* **Data Visualization:** Strong experience with Power BI, Tableau, or similar BI tools.
* **Data Handling:** Proven experience working with large-scale datasets and relational databases.
* **Excel:** Expert level in Microsoft Excel (Advanced formulas, Power Query; VBA is a plus).
* **Mathematics/Statistics:** Solid understanding of statistical analysis and data modeling techniques.

### Soft Skills & Mindset
* **Analytical Thinking:** Sharp critical thinking skills with the ability to "connect the dots" behind the data.
* **Data Storytelling:** Strong communication skills to translate technical findings into clear, actionable insights for non-technical stakeholders.
* **Attention to Detail:** High level of accuracy and meticulousness in data validation.
* **Adaptability:** Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities.
