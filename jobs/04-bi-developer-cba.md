# Business Intelligence Developer

**Company:** Commonwealth Bank  
**Location:** Sydney, NSW (Hybrid)  
**Source:** seek.com.au

---

## About the Role

Join Australia's leading bank as a Business Intelligence Developer. You'll build data solutions that power critical business decisions across retail banking, lending, and customer experience teams.

---

## Required Skills

- SQL Server, Oracle, or PostgreSQL (advanced)
- Power BI or Tableau (data visualization and dashboard development)
- Python for data manipulation and automation
- Data modeling and warehousing concepts
- ETL tools and processes

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Design and build enterprise BI dashboards and reports
- Develop and optimize data models for reporting
- Create automated data pipelines and ETL processes
- Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather requirements
- Ensure data quality and governance standards
- Provide training and support to business users

---

## Experience Requirements

- 3-5 years of experience in BI development or data engineering
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field
- Experience in financial services is preferred
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

---

## Nice to Have

- Azure or AWS data services experience
- Knowledge of machine learning and predictive analytics
- Certifications in Power BI or Tableau
- Experience with Agile delivery methodologies
