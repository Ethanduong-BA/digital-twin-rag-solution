# Data Analyst

**Company:** Atlassian  
**Location:** Sydney, NSW (Remote Friendly)  
**Source:** linkedin.com/jobs

---

## About the Role

Join our Data Analytics team to help drive data-informed decision making across the organization. You'll work with stakeholders to uncover insights and build dashboards that shape product strategy.

---

## Required Skills

- SQL (advanced queries, optimization)
- Python or R for data analysis (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Looker)
- Statistical analysis and A/B testing
- Excel and Google Sheets (advanced)

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns
- Build and maintain dashboards and automated reports
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to define metrics
- Conduct A/B tests and measure feature impact
- Present findings to stakeholders with clear data stories
- Develop KPI frameworks and tracking systems

---

## Experience Requirements

- 2-4 years of experience in data analytics or business intelligence
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Economics, or related field
- Experience with data modeling and ETL processes
- Strong communication and presentation skills

---

## Nice to Have

- Experience with dbt, Airflow, or similar tools
- Knowledge of machine learning concepts
- Familiarity with product analytics platforms (Amplitude, Mixpanel)
