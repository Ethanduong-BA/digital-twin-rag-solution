## Section 1: About the Role
The Star Entertainment Group is seeking a creative and user-centric UI/UX Designer to join their Digital & Marketing team. You will be responsible for crafting seamless digital experiences across The Star’s web and mobile platforms, ensuring that every guest interaction—from booking a suite to exploring casino rewards—is intuitive and visually stunning.

## Section 2: Responsibilities
* Design end-to-end user journeys, wireframes, and high-fidelity mockups for websites and mobile applications.
* Conduct user research and usability testing to identify pain points and validate design solutions.
* Maintain and evolve The Star’s digital design system to ensure brand consistency across all properties (Sydney, Brisbane, Gold Coast).
* Collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers to ensure technical feasibility and smooth handoffs.
* Use data-driven insights and guest feedback to iterate on existing features and improve conversion rates.
* Create interactive prototypes to communicate design intent to executive stakeholders.

## Section 3: Key Skills & Requirements

### Technical Skills
* **Design Tools:** Expert-level proficiency in Figma (required), Sketch, or Adobe Creative Suite.
* **Prototyping:** Skilled in building interactive prototypes (Protopie, Framer, or Figma).
* **UI Design:** Strong eye for typography, color theory, and layout; experience building and maintaining Design Systems.
* **UX Methodology:** Solid understanding of Information Architecture, User Flows, and Wireframing.
* **Bonus:** Basic understanding of HTML/CSS to better collaborate with Engineering teams.

### Soft Skills & Mindset
* **Empathy:** A deep focus on the "Guest First" mindset to solve complex user problems.
* **Communication:** Ability to articulate design decisions clearly to stakeholders and "sell" a vision.
* **Attention to Detail:** Precision in pixel-perfect delivery and consistency.
* **Portfolio:** A strong portfolio demonstrating your design process, from initial research to final polished UI.
