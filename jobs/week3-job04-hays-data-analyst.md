## Section 1: About the Role
This role is positioned within a leading recruitment and workforce solutions firm. The Data Analyst will support the management team by providing critical business insights, managing internal databases, and ensuring that operational performance data is accurate and accessible for strategic decision-making.

## Section 2: Responsibilities
* Collate, analyze, and interpret complex data sets to identify performance trends within the business.
* Develop and maintain internal reports and dashboards to track Consultant KPIs and revenue targets.
* Conduct regular data cleansing audits to ensure the integrity and accuracy of the CRM and internal databases.
* Provide ad-hoc analysis to senior leadership to support business planning and forecasting.
* Streamline data collection processes to improve efficiency across different departments.
* Assist in the training of staff on data entry best practices to maintain high-quality data input.

## Section 3: Key Skills & Requirements

### Technical Skills
* **Advanced Excel:** Mastery of Pivot Tables, VLOOKUPs, and complex formulas is essential.
* **Data Visualization:** Proficiency in Power BI or Tableau to create impactful visual reports.
* **Database Management:** Experience with CRM systems and an understanding of relational databases.
* **Reporting:** Proven ability to build automated reports that translate raw data into business stories.

### Soft Skills & Mindset
* **Attention to Detail:** Meticulous approach to data validation and spotting discrepancies.
* **Proactive Problem Solving:** Ability to identify gaps in data and propose logical solutions.
* **Stakeholder Management:** Strong communication skills to explain data findings to non-technical managers.
* **Time Management:** Ability to handle multiple reporting deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
