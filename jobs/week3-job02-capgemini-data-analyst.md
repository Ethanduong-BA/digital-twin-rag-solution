## Section 1: About the Role
Capgemini is a global leader in partnering with companies to transform and manage their business by harnessing the power of technology. This role sits within the Capgemini Invent or Cloud Infrastructure Services arm, focusing on delivering data-driven solutions for major clients, particularly within the utilities and energy transition sectors.

## Section 2: Responsibilities
* Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques, and provide ongoing reports.
* Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, and data analytics strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality.
* Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources and maintain databases/data systems.
* Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets to assist in business forecasting.
* Filter and "clean" data by reviewing computer reports, printouts, and performance indicators to locate and correct code problems.
* Work with management to prioritize business and information needs.

## Section 3: Key Skills & Requirements

### Technical Skills
* **Data Tools:** Strong proficiency in SQL and Python or R for data manipulation and analysis.
* **Visualization:** Hands-on experience with Power BI, Tableau, or QlikView.
* **Technical Stack:** Familiarity with cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or GCP) and ETL frameworks.
* **Domain Knowledge:** Experience in Energy, Utilities, or Government sectors is highly regarded.
* **Statistical Analysis:** Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets.

### Soft Skills & Mindset
* **Consultative Mindset:** Ability to understand client pain points and translate them into technical requirements.
* **Problem-Solving:** Proactive approach to identifying issues and proposing data-backed solutions.
* **Communication:** Excellent verbal and written communication skills for presenting insights to stakeholders.
* **Agility:** Comfortable working in fast-paced, project-based environments with evolving priorities.
