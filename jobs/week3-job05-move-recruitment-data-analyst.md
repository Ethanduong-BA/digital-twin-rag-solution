## Section 1: About the Role
Move Recruitment Solutions is a specialized agency seeking a Data Analyst to join their team in Seven Hills. This is a full-time, ongoing role focused on leveraging data to drive business growth and operational excellence within a supportive and innovative environment.

## Section 2: Responsibilities
* Collect and interpret data to identify patterns and trends that inform business strategies.
* Maintain and optimize internal databases and reporting systems to ensure data accuracy.
* Develop visual reports and dashboards to communicate key performance metrics to the leadership team.
* Perform data validation and cleansing to maintain high standards of data integrity across recruitment platforms.
* Work cross-functionally to provide data-driven insights that support sales, onboarding, and customer success teams.
* Assist in identifying process improvements through statistical analysis of operational data.

## Section 3: Key Skills & Requirements

### Technical Skills
* **Excel:** High proficiency in advanced functions (Pivot Tables, Lookups) for complex data manipulation.
* **Data Visualization:** Experience creating dashboards using tools such as Power BI or Tableau.
* **CRM/Database:** Familiarity with recruitment CRM systems or similar relational databases.
* **Analytical Tools:** Knowledge of SQL for data extraction and basic statistical modeling is highly desirable.

### Soft Skills & Mindset
* **Growth Mindset:** A proactive desire to learn, grow, and take on new challenges within the organization.
* **Attention to Detail:** Meticulous approach to data quality and validation.
* **Communication:** Ability to articulate complex data findings clearly to both technical and non-technical staff.
* **Collaborative Spirit:** A team-oriented mindset, comfortable working in a high-performing and fun-loving environment.
