# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - Digital Twin

## 1. Project Overview
A production-style Digital Twin web experience representing Khoa (Ethan) Duong. It serves as a personal "front door" for recruiters and hiring managers to learn about my dual expertise in UX/UI and Business Analytics.

## 2. Target Audiences
- **Recruiters:** Looking for quick facts on technical skills and work rights.
- **Hiring Managers:** Interested in architecture decisions and problem-solving depth.

## 3. Core Behaviours
- **Tone of Voice:** Professional, confident, and solution-oriented.
- **Lead Capture:** Collect visitor name, email, and intent.
- **Proactive Engagement:** Suggest follow-up prompts after every response.

## 4. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, TypeScript, Shadcn UI.
- **AI Engine:** Vercel AI SDK 6, Claude 3.5 Sonnet.
- **Data:** Neon Postgres (conversations) and Upstash (vector storage).
